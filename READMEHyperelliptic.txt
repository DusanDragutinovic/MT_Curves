Hyperelliptic curves in HyperellipticCurvesData are in the form:
[q(x), p(x)], where y^2 + q(x)*y = p(x). 

In HyperellipticCurvesSizeOfAutoGrp, for the i-th curve C from HyperellipticCurvesData, 
the i-th element in HyperellipticCurvesSizeOfAutoGrp is the size of automorphism group of C.
